Al Maktoum International Airport Expansion

In Progress Infrastructure Aviation

Project Overview

This major infrastructure project involves the expansion of Al Maktoum International Airport to support Dubai's position as a global aviation hub. The project includes the construction of new passenger terminals, cargo facilities, and runway extensions.

Project Scope

  • Construction of 3 new passenger terminals
  • Extension of existing runways
  • Development of cargo handling facilities
  • Installation of advanced air traffic control systems
  • Sustainable infrastructure implementation

Key Challenges

  • Maintaining airport operations during construction
  • Coordinating with multiple stakeholders
  • Implementing sustainable building practices
  • Managing large-scale logistics
